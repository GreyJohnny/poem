> # ðŸƒ Wind

---


The post-typhoon weather,  
A bit cool, a bit distant.  
The sky reflected in blue, void of life.  
&nbsp;  
Aimless, utterly bored.  
So I stop,  
A nameless sorrow,  
A nameless feeling.  
&nbsp;  
The spirit is at a loss,  
**So the body weeps for me.** &nbsp;  
So tired, so stifled, I want to cry,  
Take me away from here, quickly.  
&nbsp;  
The cold weather calms me,  
And in that calmness, I wish to vanish.  
&nbsp;  
Not that I seek to end it,  
But to be like the wind,  
**Gently brushing through the world,** **Leaving no trace behind.**

---

> *"The spirit is at a loss, so the body weeps for me."*
