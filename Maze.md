

### 迷宫

> **病情在确定的地方恶化，**
>
> **痛苦在知情的路径加深，**
>
> **我在不知道的地方大喊。**

---

*“有人跟我说这首诗应该再添一句，但我现在是写不出来了。”*


### Maze

> **The illness worsens in defined places,**
>
> **The agony deepens along known paths,**
>
> **I cry out in places unknown.**

---

*"Someone told me another line should be added to this poem, but I cannot find the words right now."*
