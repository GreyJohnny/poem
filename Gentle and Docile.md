# ğŸ•Šï¸ Gentleness / Tame

---


Mutilated wounds begin to heal,  
Layer upon layer of patches applied,  
A process unending.  
&nbsp;  
To call it loathing is imprecise; it is fear.  
&nbsp;  
Congested blood pools deeper and deeper,  
Unnoticeable, yet persistently there.  
&nbsp;  
Time caresses me,  
Rescuing me, yet more like sentencing me.  
&nbsp;  
The solid outer wall that invites admiration,  
Can shatter in a mere pulse.  
&nbsp;  
A miraculous hand restores it in an instant,  
And so it begins again.  
&nbsp;  
Pain fills the chambers of the heart,  
Yet refuses to vanish out of spite.  
&nbsp;  
**"Wait a little longer, everything will be fine."** &nbsp;  
Spoken words of love are, in truth, arrogance.  
The goddess of kindness embraces me so tightly,  
It leaves me struggling for breath.  
&nbsp;  
**"Wait a little longer, everything will be fine."** &nbsp;  
The crowd chants in unison,  
Eagerly awaiting that day.  
&nbsp;  
Compared to the fear from before,  
I dread this even more.  
&nbsp;  
Looking up,  
The judge of time is caressing me.  
**A benevolent gaze, veiling a sheer coldness.**
